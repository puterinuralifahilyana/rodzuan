History of Pertubuhan Kebajikan Anak Yatim & Asnaf Iman & Amal

Asnaf Iman & Amal Orphan Welfare Organisation is a care facility for orphans and orphans that has been in operation since 2016. The organization was founded to care for orphans and provide education that strongly emphasizes the children's morality and personalities. The organization, which has more than six years of expertise in child management, strives to assist more orphans and give them safety, education, facilities, and other necessities. To further elaborate, the organization has successfully created a profile that offers more in-depth facts about the organization to convey the whole picture. The asnaf group is anticipated to benefit from opportunities provided by this organization and other social assistance.
